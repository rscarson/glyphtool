#
# Before the first age there existed only the spirit of chaos
ti set'o pi'tsh pu O'aso'toah'ki ti'bi
#
# Its eternal task was to change and care for the sea to keep life and death as equals
O'A'ke'kar'ki A'ato toah'bi et rah'bi ti'bi ru'tahn rah'ki et toah'fel no'pe'nu aso'bi
.
#
# On the first day it raised islands from the sea, and placed life upon them
At'at set'o pi'tsh O'ifo'A'ato ti'pe'at'bi et O'aso'toah'ki rah'ki pe'ah ifo pe'bi
#
# The children of the stars, who are our ancestors, awakened in our new home
ti'si'rah O'la'si'rah'stahr O'at'at'sir'si'rah ti'ki'kar
#
# The spirit saw culture in our minds, worlds within a world, with a balance all their own
O'aso'toah'ki ti'bi si'kar'ko at'at si'kar'rah ti'tel ru'bi so'toah at'at pu'toah kar O'aso'toah'si 
.
#
# The spirit foresaw the truth. No balance could preserve all these worlds.
O'aso'toah'ki ruh ti'tel'la. no pe'et'toah O'ke'aso'bi ru'rah aso'toah
#
# But the honoured spirit could not stray from the task of balance
ru'no A'ki O'aso'toah'ke'kar no'at'bi
#
# Neither could the spirit see these inner worlds destroyed
no'et ki at'at'trois toah'fel no'ke'tel
#
# The great truth of the world was shattered and death approached
O'A'ruh'trois ti'tsh'bi et toah'fel ti'at'bi
.
#
# In its final moments, the great spirit of chaos ripped from it's heart all care for our kind
at'at O'la'la'ti'ki A'O'aso'toah'ki O'dal'si'rah at'at O'ko'ato'rah'si ti'tsh'bi
#
# The wounded spirit became the god of change and death, and was named worldsoul
fel'bi'ki E'toah ti'bi et ki trois'ki ti'si'ruh
#
# Its shattered heart became the god of immortals, and was chained beneath the seas for all time
#
# And it's tears became the demons, those gods blessed with self, as we are blessed
#
# For all time, aso're and worldsoul are bound to war, still maintaining the great task of balance for our world
